IIoT Academic Intelligence System

NexAttend

AI-Based Academic Risk Detection System

NexAttend connects biometric attendance collection with AI-based risk analysis, helping faculty identify students who may need academic support before performance drops.

Biometric Attendance

ESP32 and fingerprint sensing capture attendance events and send them to the academic data store.

02

AI Risk Prediction

A PyTorch classifier analyses attendance percentage, absence streaks, recent trends, and marks.

03

Teacher Dashboard

Faculty can review risk status, confidence, and reasons for each student in one clean interface.
